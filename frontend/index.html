About the 20-Min Index

What is a 20-Minute Neighbourhood?

The Melbourne 20-Minute Neighbourhood initiative is a planning vision for a more liveable city. The goal is simple: ensure that all Melburnians can access most of their daily needs within a 20-minute return walk from home. This represents an 800-metre radius.

Why it matters

  • Reduced car dependency and traffic congestion.
  • Increased physical activity and better health outcomes.
  • Stronger, more connected local communities.
  • Lower environmental impact and carbon footprint.

The Calculation Engine

Our Weighted Multi-Source Engine analyzes live data from Google Maps to calculate an address score. We don't just count services; we evaluate their variety and importance.

How is the rating calculated?

1

Diversity Check

Access to different categories (Food, Health, Parks, Connectivity) is critical. Having 10 cafes but no grocery store results in a lower score than having one of each.

2

Proximity & Routing

We use precise pedestrian routing. A service that is 200m away via walking paths contributes significantly more to the index than one at the 800m limit.

3

Weighted Priority

Not all services are equal. Essentials like Supermarkets and GP Services are assigned higher weights (3x) compared to lifestyle services like Cafes (1x).
