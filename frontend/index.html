    Living Locally

    The 20-minute neighbourhood concept means residents can meet most of their everyday needs within a 20-minute return walk from home — roughly an 800-metre radius. This is supported by safe cycling routes and reliable local transport.

    How We Calculate Your Score

    1

    Search 18 Essential Services

    For every address, we search Google Maps for the nearest instance of each of the 18 service types across 4 categories.

    2

    Measure Walking Distance

    We calculate the real walking distance to each service. The threshold is 800 metres — the distance of a 10-minute walk, or 20 minutes return.

    3

    Simple, Transparent Formula

    Your score = (services within 800m ÷ 18) × 10. A perfect 10.0 means all 18 services are within walking distance. No hidden weights or complex algorithms.
