About the 20-Minute Neighbourhood

The 20-minute neighbourhood concept means residents can meet most of their everyday needs within a 20-minute return walk from home — roughly an 800-metre radius. This is supported by safe cycling routes and reliable local transport.

How We Calculate Your Score

1

Search Essential Services

For every address, we search Google Maps for the nearest instance of each service type — doctors, supermarkets, parks, schools, transit, and more.

2

Measure Walking Distance

We calculate real walking distance using Google Distance Matrix. The threshold is 800 metres — the distance of a 10-minute walk, or 20 minutes return.

3

Weighted Category Scoring

Each category (Health, Food, Connectivity, etc.) is scored and weighted by importance. Services closer to you contribute more to the final 0–10 index.
