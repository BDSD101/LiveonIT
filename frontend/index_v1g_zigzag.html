    How it works

    Eight steps to understanding your neighbourhood's walkability — from your front door to your full local picture.

    Image 1
    1
    Step 1
    Discover your neighbourhood
    Enter your home address to begin. The tool locates your position within Melbourne and prepares a personalised walkability assessment aligned with the Victorian Government's 20-minute neighbourhood policy framework.
    Image 2
    2
    Step 2
    Map your 800m radius
    An 800-metre walking radius is drawn around your address, representing a genuine 20-minute return walk. This defines the zone within which essential services are assessed and scored for your area.
    Image 3
    3
    Step 3
    Score essential services
    Each category — food, health, transit, parks, education, and community — is assessed for proximity. A weighted model produces an overall walkability score reflecting how well your neighbourhood meets daily needs.
    Image 4
    4
    Step 4
    Explore the interactive map
    Dive into the dashboard with an interactive map. Toggle service layers, zoom into streets, and see exactly which facilities fall within your walking zone and which sit just beyond the boundary.
    Image 5
    5
    Step 5
    Compare by suburb
    Use the suburb leaderboard to see how your area ranks across Melbourne. Filter by city ring and service category to identify which suburbs lead in walkability and where yours stands.
    Image 6
    6
    Step 6
    Identify service gaps
    Understand exactly what's missing within your walkable zone. The gap analysis highlights low-scoring categories, giving residents, planners, and councils clear insight into where improvements are most needed.
    Image 7
    7
    Step 7
    Review supporting data
    Explore the underlying datasets powering each score. Supporting data includes crime statistics, housing density, demographic overlays, and transit frequency maps from open Victorian Government and ABS sources.
    Image 8
    8
    Step 8
    Take action locally
    Armed with your score and gap report, connect with council initiatives or share results with your community. The 20-minute neighbourhood vision becomes reality when residents and planners act on shared, transparent data.

    Living Locally

    Imagine a life where everything you need is just a short stroll away. A true 20-minute neighbourhood gives you back your time, ensuring your groceries, health services, and green spaces are within a genuine 20-minute return walk from your front door.

    Proximity to Essential Services

    Access to fresh food, healthcare, education, and banking within walking distance.

    Quality Public Spaces

    Parks, playgrounds, and recreational facilities that foster social connection.

    Transit Connectivity

    Reliable access to trains, buses, and trams connecting you to the wider city.
